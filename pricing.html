Frequently asked

Pricing, explained.

Anything we haven't covered? Ask us on a call we'll send you back in writing.

Why don't you publish prices?

Because every brand has a different starting point, audience and budget. Quoting a one-size-fits-all number would mean over-charging some and under-serving others. We'd rather scope properly and quote honestly.

How fast can I get a proposal?

Within 48 hours of our discovery call. A written one-pager covering what we'd do, in what order, the investment range and when we could start.

Do you cancel at 30 days?

Yes no annual lock-in, no exit fee. 30 days' notice in writing and we wind the work down cleanly. We'd rather earn next month.

Who actually does the work?

A small, hands-on team. Strategy, paid media and analytics are run in-house. For design, video and engineering we pull in trusted collaborators you'll meet everyone in week one. Nothing is outsourced overseas or white-labelled.

How is ad spend handled?

You pay Meta, Google and LinkedIn directly from your card. We never mark up ad spend or pass it through our books. Our fee covers the work; spend is spend.

Can you start in a week?

Often, yes depending on capacity. Projects usually kick off within ten working days; retainers within two to three weeks.
