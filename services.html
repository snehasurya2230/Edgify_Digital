Standalone

Just need a logo?

Sometimes you don't need a full brand system, you just need a mark that looks the part. We do standalone logo design as a fixed-scope project.

30 minutes on what the business does, who it's for, and what the logo needs to do (sign-board, app icon, letterhead).
